History of Smoothie Bowl

Smoothie Bowl was founded in 2023 by Jaycob Tharp. The company has become a safe place for anyone to feel welcomed. Smoothie Bowl strives to not only be the best meal replacement but to also be a home for everyone. The company has plans to expand their business to college campuses, airports, and larger cities all around the United States.

Smooth your way into a healthier life style!

Smoothie Bowl is designed to help you transition unhealthy eating habits into a meal replacement full of vital nutrients. There is a smoothie for everyone. Smoothie bowl even allows customers to make their own custom smoothie.
